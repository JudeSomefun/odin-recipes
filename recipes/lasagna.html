Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of my favorite dishes, hands down. I first remember having it when my dad would cook it for me and my sister in apartment in Harlem. It was really fine dining.

It consists of several layers of meat sauce, cheese, and pasta. That process is repeated once or twice within the cooking pan.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna accoridng to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
